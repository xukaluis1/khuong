Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to make an appointment before visiting the salon?
It is not mandatory, but booking in advance will help you avoid long waits.
2. Does the salon use safe products for nails?
We only use high-quality products that are safe for your health.
3. Can I request a custom nail design?
Of course! Bring your favorite design, and we will create it for you.
4. Does the salon offer nail services for groups or events?
Yes! Contact us in advance to book and receive group discounts.
5. How often should I get my nails redone?
Depending on the service, but usually every 2-3 weeks to maintain beauty.
